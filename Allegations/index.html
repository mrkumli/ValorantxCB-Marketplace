Allegations

If you have any of the following Allegations(An issue/misunderstanding without a proof) against a Seller, it will not be claimed/accepted or taken action upon by the admins without Solid Proof:

Payment Issue

If you have an allegation of the payment (sent to the seller) or do not have the date of dealing(or if it is too old to be accepted/or proved true), the admins will not be responsible/take action upon it

False Report

If the Buyer/Seller reports an allegation (which upon investigations) the Buyer/Seller is found guilty, He/She will be terminated permanently/temporarily or will be suspended from the group

Proof

Any allegation without a solid proof will not be accepted/approved by the admins. Therefore (solid) proof must be collected in regard to the allegation before reporting it to the admins

Physical Appearance/Dealing

Physical (face to face) dealing with the Buyer/Seller is not supported by the group and therefore the Group Admins will not be responsible if an allegation is caused due to/through physical dealings

Physical Accuses

The Seller/Buyer must not approach each other's location/house in case of an allegation. It must be solved via the Group Admins only! Physical accuses/approach will not be tolerated and can result in perma ban/suspend or will be dealt through legal forces if the situation gets worse/out of control

Public Post about Allegation

The Buyer/Seller must not post/comment regarding the allegation. The allegation must be tried to be resolved with the dealer first, then with the admins. The admins will deal with the concerned person accordingly. Any post regarding the Allegation will not be approved/or if approved will be taken down immediately.
If the Situation gets worse and the allegation is already being dealt/or have been dealt via the admins, the Buyer/Seller will be given permission to post a -rep post/comment about the concerned person.

Involvement of Other Persons

The Seller/Buyer must not involve any third member/person in the dealing. The prucahse/dealing must be made and kept private between the Seller & Buyer. Involvement of 3rd person in the deal from Seller or Buyer's side will not be tolerated

No Phone Calls

No phone calls are allowed. You must message the concerned person and he/she will respond to it when free, phone calls should not be made to the Seller/Buyer and especially to the admins.

No Spamming

Spamming is not allowed whether it is of phone calls or messages. You must wait patiently for the Buyer/Seller/Admin to reply to you. Messages/Calls should not be spammed, or the spamming member will receive a temp/perm ban on the group

Verification Status

The admins will deal with any allegation made against the Seller.But, If the Seller is unverified, the admins cannot guarantee the seller's repute. The admins take responsibility of the ValorantxCB Verified Sellers only as their requests have been approved by the admin team themselves and that the admins have satisfactory positive vouches and reps of the Seller.
If any misconduct is caused by a Verified Seller, the admins will claim the vouches from the Seller's vouchers adn return the lost amount to the buyer.
