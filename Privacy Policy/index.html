Valorant x CB has some Privacy Policies and Terms & Conditions which must be kept in mind and read thoroughly. These are as followed:

Ownership

The products available for sale/trade on The Selling Section are not owned by Valorant x CB. The products belong to their respective Sellers

Confirmation

The products were listed for sale with consent of the Sellers through their acceptation comment on one of the Announcement Posts (or Some were informed via Personal Messages) i.e the seller is aware that his/her Product is being listed on the website for sale/purchase

Payment

Valorant x CB is not responsible for misuse/mishandling of Payment. Buyer must pay the Seller on his exact and accurate Payment Address. Incase of Loss/Waste of money due to Mishandle/Misconduct of Payment Options, the Admins will not take any action upon it since it is clearly a Human Error and therefore the amount cannot be recovered by the Admin

Pricing

The Seller is instructed to make a fair price of his/her product (apart from the Currencies as they vary day by day). Negotiation varies person to person, Price is not set Negotiable on the Listings page. Therefore the Buyer has no right to complain about the price.
A small fee (of PKR 100-200/1 USD) will be charged by the group to list a Seller's item on the market. The collected fee amount will only used to pay the Hosting & Domain bills of the website and the Admins cannot use it personally.

Admin Support

Admin Support will only be available if the Buyer/Seller has Proof (Read Pre/Post Selling Info for more details). If an issue persists or if there is a query/complain/advice or any other issue, Contact the Admins via Personal Message (DM)

Dealing Platform

Dealings must only be done via Personal Message (DM) through Facebook Messenger. Dealings done outside the instructed platform are not governed/related to Valorant x CB and if an issue persists the Admins will not respond to it
The members will be able to contact the sellers through our Forum, but for now it is still under development

Listing during Admin Investigation

If the Admin Investigation is being held on over an issue/scam/misunderstanding, the Listing will be taken down/muted from the website until the issue is resolved

Verified Sellers List

To qualify for the Verified Sellers List, one must have atleast 20 +ve Reps and 25 Vouches minimum (each vouch >= 400pkr atleast) on his Rep/Vouch post of the group. The seller must have 30 vouches atleast (each vouch >=500 pkr atleast) if he/she wants to add a listing(ad) of >=12k pkr on the marketplace. The Seller will then be given Rating (out of 5 stars) which is based on:

  • No. of Positive Reps & Vouches
  • Active Selling Time on the Group/Market
  • Public Response
If all the above steps are comprised(completed), submit a request to the admins via DM or mail us the credentials at:
dealings@valorantxcb.com
If the application/request is approved by the admins, the Seller will then be listed on the Valorant x CB Verified Sellers Section and will have a verified varification status on each of their items listed. Recommended Sellers will be selected by the Admin Team when they surpass their reputation on the group and the above mentioned points.
Note that Rep/Vouch post must only be of the group Valorant x CB. Posts from other groups will not be accepted.
Seller can use other groups as a reference, but selections will only be based on Valorant x CB Rep/Vouch post.

Agreement

The Buyer must read thoroughly and accept the Privacy Policy and the Pre/Post Selling Info before making a deal/purchase
