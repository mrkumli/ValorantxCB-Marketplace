Requisition

The following points (requisitions) must be kept in mind, agreed upon and accepted by the Buyer before making purchase/dealings

Age Limit

The Buyer must be aged enough to make Online Payments/Dealings without their parent's consent.

Group Member

The buyer must be a member of the group Valorant x CB in order to be eligible for making a deal/purchase via the website and must be a member of the group for atleast 1 month in order to list anything on the Selling Section.

Payment Options

The Buyer has to make a deal with the seller via Online Payments

Payment Option vary on Buyer to Buyer. Some common Payment options are:

  1. EasyPaisa
  2. Bank Transfer
  3. Nayapay
  4. JazzCash
  5. UPaisa
  6. SadaPay
  7. Crypto

Date of Creation

If the Date of Creation of the Product/Item is older than 8-12 days, the prices must have varied or the product could have been sold.
DM seller about the item to confirm its availability and latest price

Facebook Account

The Buyer must have Facebook Messenger to be able to contact the Seller regarding the purchase as dealings can only be done via peronal DMs with the Seller.
We're trying our level-best to overercome this issue too to make the experience better for our visitors. But for now dealings are limited to Personal Messages (DMs) only

Facebook Account Confirmation

The Buyer must confirm the originaltiy of the Seller's Account and match the Facebook ID of the Seller from the Valorant x CB Verified Sellers Section before make a payment/dealing

Agreement

The Buyer must accept the Privacy Policy and read the Pre/Post Selling Info thoroughly before making a deal/purchase
