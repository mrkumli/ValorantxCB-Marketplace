Rules

Make sure to go through the following Rules thoroughly

Selling/Puchasing/Trading

Seling/Puchasing/Trading of Accounts, Currencies or any other things is not allowed on the Group. Any of the Selling/Purchasing/Trading(Related) will not be approved. Dealings can only be made via the website through Personal Messages

Support through Admins

If you have any issue regarding a scam/misunderstanding/complain, or about the group or the website, Contact the Admins directly.
Contact the Admins via Facebook Messenger or Mail us at:
info@valorantxcb.com
If a member is found guilty in a scam case and refuses to return the lost amount to the concerned person, a First Information Report(FIR) will be filed at the local Police Station for the concerned member.
Note that the concerned member will be given only 3 days and if no response is received by the Admins, the FIR will be filed.

If the Member/Seller has genuine reasons for the scam conducted and is willing to return the lost(scammed) amount, mail us the details at: dealings@valorantxcb.com asap with *solid proof* and a written statement in black and white that he/she is willing to return the lost amount.

Physical Appearance/Dealing

Physical (face to face) dealing with a member is not supported by the group and therefore the Group Admins will not be responsible if a misunderstanding is caused due to/through physical meetings

Physical Accuses

Members must not approach each other's location/house in case of any issue. It must be solved via the Group Admins only! Physical accuses/approach will not be tolerated and can result in perma ban/suspend or will be dealt through legal forces if the situation gets worse/out of control

Public Posts

The Buyer/Seller must not post/comment anything related to any dealing/issue on the group until the deal is done/or until the issue is solved by the admins.

Involvement of Other Persons

Members must not involve any third member/person in a dealing. The prucahse/dealing must be made and kept private between the Seller & Buyer. Involvement of 3rd person in the deal from Seller or Buyer's side will not be tolerated and allowed

No Phone Calls

No phone calls are allowed. You must message the concerned person and he/she will respond to it when free, phone calls should not be made to a member and especially to the admins.

No Spamming

Spamming is not allowed whether it is of phone calls or messages. You must wait patiently for the concerned person to reply to you. Messages/Calls should not be spammed, or the spamming member will receive a temp/perm ban on the group
